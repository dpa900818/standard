About Standard Club

We are a specialist marine and energy insurer known for our diverse membership, pioneering loss prevention expertise and attractive pricing.

Find out more

Core P&I

Our core P&I insurance is competitively priced, secure and backed by the highest levels of service. 

Defence

We provide attractively priced FD&D insurance that will protect you against legal and other costs of maritime dispute resolution.

Extensions to Core P&I

Our expert underwriters can give you a tailored, competitive quote on any maritime insurance need, however unusual.

Offshore & Renewables 

Contact us for competitively priced liability insurance in the offshore oil, gas and alternative energy industries. 

Coastal & Inland

Discover our tailored mutual and fixed-premium liability insurance for vessels operating in European inland, harbour and coastal waters.

Traders

For commodity traders who charter ships we can provide a complete Traders' Liability policy.

Charterers

We cover charterers for P&I, cargo owners’ legal liability, damage to hull, consortium damage to hull and non-vessel operation common carrier.

Kidnap & Ransom

If your ships are trading in high-risk piracy areas, we can cover you for kidnap and ransom costs.

War Risks

Our war risks insurance protects ships against loss or malicious damage by hostile third parties not covered by hull or P&I insurance.

Strike & Delay

Our attractively priced and flexible strike & delay insurance covers the cost of delays to ships arising from both shoreside and shipside events.
